To ensure you can complete a payment plan, we'd like to ask about your income and spending. We will use this information to calculate your disposable income.

You don't need any specific documents to answer the questions. Although, you might find it helpful to get bank statements or accounts before you continue.

How we calculate the plan
We expect you to pay 50% of your disposable income towards the plan. We expect 50% rather than 100% because we want:

  • your arrangement to be sustainable
  • for you to be able to manage any unexpected changes in spending

You may wish to pay more than 50% to reduce the interest you pay.
